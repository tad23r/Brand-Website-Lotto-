History

Founded in 1973 by Gino and Lamberto Marchetti, Lotto Sport Italia started with tennis footwear and quickly expanded into soccer and running gear. Known for its innovative designs and quality, Lotto gained prominence through sports sponsorships. Today, it remains a global player in sportswear, focusing on performance and sustainability.
