Lost, held, or damaged packages

Ownership of packages turned over to USPS/UPS/Fedex transfers to the buyer. LottoSports.com is not responsible for lost, held, or damaged packages if a claim is denied by courier service. We are not responsible for mis-delivery errors via carrier, or incorrect shipping info.

Do you ship to P.O. boxes?

LottoSports.com does not offer shipping to P.O. Box addresses. We apologize for any inconvenience.

What locations do you ship to?

LottoSports.com ships to all 50 states including U.S. Territories provided that it is a physical address. We do not ship to PO Box, or APO/FPO or Internationally currently (APO military address + FPO Fleet Post Off which is also associated with military)

Do you ship to Alaska, Hawaii or Puerto Rico?

Please note that Alaska, Hawaii, and Puerto Rico addresses are shipping with Expedited shipping options that use air carriers. We apologize for any inconvenience.
